What is a Banana?

A banana is a long, curved fruit that grows in clusters and has soft pulpy flesh and yellow skin when ripe. It is one of the most popular fruits in the world.

Health Benefits

  • Rich in potassium
  • Good source of energy
  • Helps digestion
  • Supports heart health

Fun Facts

Did you know? Bananas are technically berries, and the banana plant is actually an herb!
